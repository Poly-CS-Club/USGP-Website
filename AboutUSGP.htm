About USGP

About USGP:

Undergraduate Student Government Polytechnic (USGP) serves the undergraduate student body of the Arizona State University Polytechnic Campus. USGP represents the concerns and needs of the students to the ASU Administration, the Arizona Board of Regents, and the State Legislature. USGP has a wide variety of programs and services including the Bike Co-Op. USG Advocates for college affordability, registers students to vote, and insures that students have a voice in campus and university decisions.

USG also provides club funding for organizations across campus through SFAB Appropriations. Please see the SFAB portion of this website to learn more about the SFAB Appropriations process.
